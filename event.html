Events

We conduct various events encouraging the spirit of hacking and promoting free software across the country. Monthly FOSS Meetup, IndiaFOSS conference and FOSS Hack, to name a few.

Community Events

We support various events encouraging the spirit of hacking and promoting free software across the country. The FOSS United communities in different cities build their own niches by hosting events that foster the spirit of FOSS.

Monthly Meetups

A city community meets in-person on the second or last Saturday of every month. where FOSS contributors are invited to talk about their contributions to FOSS, and have open discussions.

City FOSS Conferences

Once a year, each city organises a FOSS Conference to celebrate the local FOSS creators and contributors.

Hackathons

Intensive tech specific events where enthusiasts collaboratively develop FOSS, showcasing innovation and fostering the hacking and tinkering spirit.

Linux Installation Parties

Tech-focused gatherings where enthusiasts come together to collaboratively install, learn and explore Linux. We support "End Of 10" campaign, supported by KDE.
